Juddy

Juddy is a multi-instrumentalist who grew up in the era of 90s video games enriched by the masterful compositions of Nobuo Uematsu and Junichi Masuda.

In late 2021, Juddy released I Am a New Tie Wearing, inspired by sonnyboy and his album Love Junkie, Tie Wearing was an exercise in electronica, a large leap from his origins in power pop and punk.

In early 2022, Juddy and sonnyboy formed The Chaddington Street Milk Collective and released Crescendo Ultima Morendo, combining their mutual love of JRPGs and uniting their lo-fi chill vibes with pop sensibilities.

Juddy would appear on the sonnyboy sequel album Love Junkie 2 released June 2022, featuring on the tracks Sweet Lavender, and Gone Again.

Juddy's latest EP Shadows of a Cityscape is a retro-wave journey from Atari to Stranger Things full of bright lights and dystopia, it was released July 22 and features contributions from sonnyboy on the tracks Raving Mad and Rear View.
