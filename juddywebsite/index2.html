Juddy

A newcomer to the retrowave scene with a background in power-pop and dabblings in punk, Juddy has had a love affair with video game soundtracks by Nobuo Uematsu and Junichi Masuda since the late 90s.

Juddy released I Am a New Tie Wearing in late 2021. Inspired by sonnyboy and his album Love Junkie, Tie Wearing was an exercise in electronic minimalism, using mostly an electric piano, a synth bass and an 808 drum kit as the foundation to foray into electronica.

In early 2022, Juddy and sonnyboy formed The Chaddington Street Milk Collective and released Crescendo Ultima Morendo, combining their mutual love of JRPGs and uniting their lo-fi chill vibes with pop sensibilities.

Juddy would appear on the sonnyboy sequel album Love Junkie 2 released June 2022, featuring on the tracks Sweet Lavender, and Gone Again.

Juddy's latest EP Shadows of a Cityscape is a retro-wave journey from Atari to Stranger Things full of bright lights and dystopia, an introduction exploring the dreary neon future that Tron or Blade Runner promised. It was released July 22 and features contributions from sonnyboy on the tracks Raving Mad and Rear View.
